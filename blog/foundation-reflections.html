Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

It is because learning the core skills helps develop your emotional intelligence and allows the students to build interpersonal tools to cope with the high-stress, intensity-driven learning style in the Bootcamp and, along the way, develop tools that are transferable in your professional career.
